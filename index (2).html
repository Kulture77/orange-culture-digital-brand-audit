Strategic Recommendations
Six priority actions · prioritised by impact and feasibility
07
01

Launch Official TikTok Channel — Now

Create @orangecultureng on TikTok immediately. Build three content pillars: (1) Atelier & making-of — raw footage from the Lagos studio, fabric sourcing, hand-finishing; (2) "Who Wears OC?" — cuts of Davido, Ncuti Gatwa, and other co-signs styled in the brand; (3) Designer voice — Adebayo speaking directly to fashion identity, masculinity, and Nigerian culture. TikTok's median brand engagement rate (27.6%) is 3× Instagram's. Every week without a channel is a competitor advantage being handed away.

Urgent
Highest impact
02

Fix Mobile Website Speed

The 48/100 mobile PageSpeed score is actively suppressing Google rankings and costing customers at the moment of purchase intent. Priority fixes: convert all product images to WebP format, implement lazy loading, install a CDN (Cloudflare), and audit the WooCommerce plugin stack for render-blocking scripts. A one-second improvement in load time correlates to a 7% increase in conversion rate — this is recoverable revenue.

Urgent
High impact
03

Launch an OC Editorial Content Hub

Create a brand editorial section on orangeculture.com.ng — long-form pieces on the OC story, collection concept notes, and the designer's cultural commentary. This builds Domain Authority through earned backlinks, targets high-intent SEO terms ("African luxury fashion," "gender-fluid Nigerian designer"), and converts press-referred visitors into buyers. Two editorial pieces per month is the minimum effective cadence.

Q3 2026
Med-High impact
04

Diaspora Paid Social — London, NYC, Paris

Activate Meta Ads targeting Nigerian diaspora in the three cities where OC is already stocked. Use celebrity UGC-style creative. Start at £500–£1,000/month for testing. Diaspora audiences carry the highest willingness to pay a premium for Nigerian design — this is the highest-intent purchaser segment in the existing audience and it is almost entirely unactivated by paid media today.

Q3 2026
Med-High impact
05

Activate Google Shopping via WooCommerce

Connect the existing WooCommerce store to Google Merchant Center using the native Google for WooCommerce plugin. This is a one-time technical setup that puts OC products in front of active buyers searching Google for fashion items. Estimated incremental e-commerce sessions: +25–40%. The store infrastructure already exists — this is a missed channel, not a build.

Q3 2026
Medium impact
06

Build Email Engine with Drop-Waitlist Model

Install Klaviyo (native WooCommerce integration) and build three automated flows: a welcome sequence for new subscribers, a pre-launch waitlist mechanic for upcoming collections, and a post-purchase story sequence. The drop-waitlist model — used to great effect by Ashluxe — drives urgency and repeat engagement. Email marketing returns an average £36 per £1 spent in fashion e-commerce.

Q4 2026
Medium impact
Tools Guide
How to execute each recommendation · platform by platform
08
Social Analytics
Meta Business Suite
Free
Native Instagram and Facebook analytics — follower growth, reach, engagement, and demographic breakdowns.
How to use: Connect @orangecultureng. Monitor weekly reach, impressions, and saves — saves are the highest purchase-intent signal on Instagram. Review the Audience tab monthly for demographic shifts.
TikTok Growth
TikTok Creator Studio
Free
Native analytics and content management for TikTok. Video-level performance, follower analytics, trending sounds, and audience insights.
How to use: After creating @orangecultureng, switch to Business Account. Use Creator Search Insights to find trending Nigerian fashion hashtags. Post 3–4× weekly to build algorithmic momentum in the first 30 days.
SEO — Baseline
Google Search Console
Free
Shows exactly which search queries drive traffic to orangeculture.com.ng, click-through rates, and indexing health.
How to use: Verify site ownership. Filter queries by positions 8–20 — these are the quick-win ranking opportunities. Submit updated sitemap after any site changes or new editorial content.
Web Analytics
Google Analytics 4
Free
Full conversion funnel tracking — sessions, product views, add-to-cart, purchases, and revenue by traffic source.
How to use: Install GA4 WooCommerce plugin. Build Funnel reports to see where visitors drop off between product page → cart → checkout. Track which traffic source generates the highest revenue per visit.
Technical SEO
Google PageSpeed Insights
Free
Audits website performance on mobile and desktop, scoring 0–100 with specific technical recommendations.
How to use: Enter orangeculture.com.ng. Prioritise the red "Opportunities" — almost always image compression and render-blocking JavaScript. Share the full report directly with the web developer; the fixes are specific and actionable.
Content Scheduling
Later
Freemium
Schedule and auto-publish across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and X from one dashboard with performance analytics.
How to use: Build a rolling 2-week content calendar. Use the AI-powered "Best Time to Post" feature. Track which post formats drive the most link-in-bio clicks to measure e-commerce intent from social.
SEO Intelligence
Semrush
$139/mo
Professional SEO suite for keyword research, competitor gap analysis, backlink audits, and site health scoring.
How to use: Enter ash-luxe.com in Competitor Analysis to see which keywords Ashluxe ranks for that OC doesn't. Run the Keyword Gap report to build the editorial content calendar. Start with the 7-day free trial.
Email Marketing
Klaviyo
Freemium
The industry-leading email platform for fashion e-commerce. Deep WooCommerce integration with automated flows built for retail brands.
How to use: Install WooCommerce plugin. Build Drop Waitlist flow first: sign up → teaser email series → launch day email. Then build abandoned cart recovery. Free up to 250 contacts — start immediately at no cost.
Paid Advertising
Meta Ads Manager
Budget required
Facebook and Instagram paid campaigns with precise audience targeting by geography, interests, and custom audiences.
How to use: Create Advantage+ campaign targeting Nigerian diaspora — geo: London, NYC, Paris; interest: Nigerian fashion, African culture. Use celebrity-wearing-OC video as creative. Start at $500/month, scale on ROAS ≥2.5×.
Google Shopping
Google Merchant Center
Free (ad spend)
Gets OC products appearing in Google Shopping results, synced automatically from the existing WooCommerce catalog.
How to use: Create Merchant Center account → install "Google for WooCommerce" plugin → verify domain → submit product feed. Then run Performance Max campaigns in Google Ads. This is a 2–3 hour technical setup, not a build.
Influencer Intelligence
Modash
Freemium
Influencer discovery and vetting. Search by niche, audience location, engagement rate, and follower authenticity score.
How to use: Filter for Nigerian fashion influencers with 10K–100K followers and engagement above 4%. Prioritise those with 40%+ audience in Lagos, London, or NYC — where OC is stocked and can actually convert the sale.
Brand Monitoring
Google Alerts + Mention
Free / Freemium
Real-time monitoring of every online mention of Orange Culture — press, blogs, social conversations, and competitor coverage.
How to use: Set alerts for "Orange Culture," "orangecultureng," and "Adebayo Oke-Lawal." Every press mention without a link to the site is a backlink opportunity — reach out to editors requesting they add the URL. This alone can meaningfully lift DA over 6–12 months.
